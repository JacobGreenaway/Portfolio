Welcome to Unearth

An interactive sandpit experience which brings the imaginations of children into the tangible.

Take a tour.

Unearth is an physical interaction design project which aims to facilitate bonding experiences between parents and children. Children are given free reign to create a world with their hands, and parents are given the opportunity to interact with their children in a way which bridges generational gap.
